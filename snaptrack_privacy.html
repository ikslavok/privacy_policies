Privacy Policy for SnapTrack

Last updated: December 19, 2024

1. Introduction

SnapTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Information Collection and Use

SnapTrack is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information from our users.

2.1 Data Storage

All data created and stored by SnapTrack remains exclusively on your device, including:

  • Photos taken with the camera
  • Checklists and checkbox items
  • Text and number entries
  • Project information
  • Progress tracking data

This data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

3. Camera Permission

SnapTrack requires camera permission to function properly. The camera is used to:

  • Take photos for tracking your progress
  • Capture images associated with your projects

Important: All photos taken through SnapTrack are stored locally on your device. We do not access, transmit, or store these photos on any external servers.

4. Local Authentication

SnapTrack may use your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint, face recognition) or PIN/password for securing access to the app. This authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We do not collect or store any biometric data.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screen protection
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using the app's built-in security features if available

6. Third-Party Services

SnapTrack does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app is completely private.

7. Data Sharing and Export

SnapTrack includes features to share or export your data (such as sharing photos or creating GIFs). When you use these features:

  • Data is only shared when you explicitly choose to do so
  • Sharing is done through your device's standard sharing mechanisms
  • You control what is shared and with whom

8. Children's Privacy

SnapTrack does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

9. Data Backup and Deletion

Your data may be included in your device's backup system (such as iCloud or Google Backup). To permanently delete your data:

  • Delete items within the app, or
  • Uninstall the application from your device

Please note that data in your device backups will be retained according to your device backup settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

12. Your Rights

Since SnapTrack does not collect or process any personal data, standard data protection rights (such as access, rectification, or deletion requests) are not applicable. You have full control over your data as it resides entirely on your device.
